## Memorize 52 Cards

Memorize the order of a shuffled 52-card deck:

1. map each card to a word
2. tell a story about 4 cards upon each of 13 body parts (clockwise)
   * head
   * right shoulder
   * right elbow
   * right wrist
   * right hip
   * right knee
   * right ankle
   * left ankle
   * left knee
   * left hip
   * left wrist
   * left elbow
   * left shoulder
